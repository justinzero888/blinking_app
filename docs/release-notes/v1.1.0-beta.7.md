## What's New

### 📊 Insights Phase 2 — Writing Stats (CT1)

New "Writing Stats" card on the Insights tab tracks your journaling habits:

- **Average Words** per entry — see how much you typically write
- **Most Active Day** — which day of the week you journal most
- **Peak Writing Hour** — your most productive time of day
- All stats filtered by selected time range (week/month/year)

### 🏷️ Insights Phase 2 — Tag-Mood Correlation (CT3)

Discover how your tags relate to your emotional state:

- Each tag shows its **mood score** (😊=5 to 😡=1)
- Minimum 3 entries per tag for statistical relevance
- Tags with the most positive/negative associations surfaced prominently
- Helps identify which activities, people, or contexts affect your mood

### ✅ Insights Phase 2 — Checklist Analytics (CT2)

New analytics for daily checklists:

- **Total Lists** created and **Completion Rate** (%)
- **Carry-Forward Rate** — how often items roll over to the next day
- **Top Checklist Item** — your most frequently used to-do
- All stats filtered by selected time range

### 🎨 Hero Row Overflow Fix

- Fixed 4th hero card being clipped on iPhone screens
- Hero stats row now scrolls horizontally when content exceeds screen width
