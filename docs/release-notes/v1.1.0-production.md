# Blinking v1.1.0 — Production Release Notes

> **Release Date:** 2026-05-16
> **Build:** 1.1.0+40
> **Platforms:** iOS App Store + Google Play
> **Type:** Production Release (stable)

---

## Release Summary

Blinking Notes 1.1.0 is the first production release. A private journaling app with AI-powered reflections, habit tracking, and mood analytics. No social media, no accounts, no distraction — just you and your memories.

---

## What's Included

### 📝 Journaling
- Freeform and checklist entries with emotion picker (10 emotions) and custom tags
- Media attachments (image) with automatic compression (1920px, q85)
- Full search and tag/date filtering
- Private tag support — entries marked with 🔒 AI Secrets excluded from AI context
- One checklist per day with user-prompted carry-forward from yesterday
- Past-date entries are view-only (preserves memory integrity)

### 🤖 AI Reflections
- **4 AI Personas:** Kael (📝 Factual), Elara (🌿 Warm), Rush (⚡ Unfiltered), Marcus (⚔️ Stoic)
- Multi-turn chat with Save Reflection
- Single-turn lens-based reflections (Zengzi's Three self-examination)
- Mood Moment postures via emoji jar "Ask AI"
- All numeric quota language removed — AI feels unlimited during preview
- Server-configurable AI keys: model switching and key rotation without app updates
- Multi-key failover: automatic fallback on rate limits, auth failures, timeouts
- Streaming for perceived speed (0.6s first token)

### 📋 Habit Tracking
- Build/Do/Reflect tabs for habit management
- Four scheduling modes: Daily, Weekly, Scheduled (one-time), Adhoc (on-demand)
- Streak tracking with grace period
- Calendar view with routine checklists and emoji badges
- Category-based organization with auto-detection
- Habit import/export (JSON)

### 📊 Insights
- Yearly emoji jar carousel — browse your emotional year at a glance
- Summary charts: note count, habit completion rate, mood trend, top tags
- Writing stats: average words, most active day, peak writing hour
- Tag-Mood correlation: which activities affect your mood
- Checklist analytics: completion rate, carry-forward rate
- AI-Generated Insights: LLM-powered analysis with rule-based fallback

### 🔄 Backup & Restore
- ZIP export/import with progress bar
- Media-exclude toggle (text-only backup ~200KB)
- Persona and AI configuration included in backup
- Cross-device data portability

### 🌐 Chorus Social (Optional)
- Post individual entries to the Blinking Chorus community
- Anonymous by default — share only what you choose
- Per-entry opt-in — your journal stays private

### 🎨 UI & UX
- Bilingual UI: English and Simplified Chinese
- M3 Onboarding — 3-screen first-launch flow
- Floating robot AI companion (bobbing + pulsing animation)
- Contextual FAB (changes per tab)
- My Day / Moments / Routines / Insights / Settings tab navigation
- Dark mode support

### 💰 Purchase Model
- **21-day free preview** — full access, 3 AI reflections/day
- **Blinking Pro — $19.99 one-time purchase** (non-consumable, Family Sharing supported)
- No subscriptions, no recurring billing
- RevenueCat IAP with sandbox testing verified on both platforms
- Server-authoritative entitlement with local offline fallback
